Built for the management and access layer

The AS4625-54T is a 1U fixed-configuration open networking switch for management and access roles across the data center, enterprise, and campus. Forty-eight 1GbE copper ports connect server and storage management interfaces in-rack, while six 1G/10G SFP+ uplinks aggregate cleanly to the network core. Pre-loaded with ONIE, it boots the network operating system you choose — open-source or commercial — on Broadcom Trident III silicon delivering full line-rate L2/L3 forwarding. Dual hot-swappable 80 Plus Platinum power supplies and 2+1 redundant fans keep it running through a component failure, and TPM 2.0 with optional UEFI Secure Boot anchors a hardware root of trust.

Right-sized port mix

48 RJ45 management/access ports plus 6 SFP+ uplinks supporting 1GE and 10GE — one box for the whole rack.

x86 control plane

Intel Atom C3508 4-core COMe module with 8 GB DDR4 and 32 GB m.2 SSD runs modern NOS workloads comfortably.

Hardware root of trust

TPM 2.0 (SPI) with options to enable UEFI Secure Boot for verified, tamper-resistant boot chains.

No single point of failure

1+1 redundant, load-sharing, hot-swappable 150 W PSUs and fixed 2+1 fans in front-to-back or back-to-front airflow.

Open by default

ONIE installer ships on every unit, so you choose open-source or commercial NOS — including SONiC and Exaware ExaNOS.

Lean power envelope

Typical draw from just 33.5 W (no optics) keeps operating cost and thermal load low across dense deployments.
